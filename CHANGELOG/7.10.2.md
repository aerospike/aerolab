# [v7.10.2](https://github.com/aerospike/aerolab/releases/tag/7.10.2)

_Release Date: April 20th, 2026_

**Release Notes:**

* `cluster partition conf`: clamp the computed `partition-tree-sprigs` to the Aerospike-documented range `[256, 268435456]` (power of 2), and refuse to write a config when even 256 sprigs do not fit the configured `mounts-budget`. Previously, small pi-flash partitions could cause aerolab to emit `partition-tree-sprigs 128`, which Aerospike rejects before logging (very painful to debug).
* `agi` ingest triggers would cause stack trace crashes in certain corner case scenarios due to improper nil check.
* Default to hyperdisks on GCP for instances that do not support `pd`.
